Mogra

Mogra is an experimental Gujarati and Latin typeface that borrows heavily from street hand-lettering and broad-nibbed markers. While Mogra gujarati looks to emphasise the flamboyant gujarati out-stroke with size, weight and flair, the latin borrows the indic pen angle to seamlessly compliment the Gujarati, retaining emphasis on the tail.

The project files and documentation are shared on GitHub. Mogra will soon be published by Google Fonts.
